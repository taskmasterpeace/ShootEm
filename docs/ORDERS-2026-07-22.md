## NEW items born from the answers ➕

1. **The standing reticle is still wrong** (the thing he ended on): it *twists when you turn* —
   it must rotate WITH the player and stay a stable ground aim-mark so a shot passes through
   the circle in the 3D world. Make it slightly **opaque, overlay-like**. Fix FIRST, then the
   hide-behind-cover setting rides on it.
2. **Runway + aircraft carrier + jet fuel.** Planes need a runway (or VTOL exceptions);
   runway length ↔ carrier deck length (the carrier moves planes — important); **jet fuel is a
   real resource**. Plus **sortie stats** (flights, kills per sortie, land-vs-air kills) —
   the game is stat-heavy, lean into it.
3. **The base buildings confirmed** (repeats + extends META): vehicle build/repair shop ·
   infantry resupply · personal weapons locker · cloning vat + print library · hospital ·
   helipad · **runway** (new).
4. **Rangefinder / measured distance** — this is what "meters, two users" meant: players hear
   "2u, 9u" but can't MEASURE. A rangefinder readout; plus a **laser-designated aerial strike**
   (paint the target, the strike arrives) and **C4 with a user-set timer**.
5. **Skip the bleed-out** — when downed with nobody near, one input skips straight to respawn.
6. **Unarmed/melee lock-on** — when close and unarmed (or melee-armed), the character faces
   the target automatically and closes distance cleanly (fixes the too-close turning jank).
   Grapple resolution weighs strength/weight. Needs a **harness scenario** to practice.
   (Creative latitude granted.)
7. **Air combat fixes:** missiles slightly faster (air-to-air) · a dedicated **flare key**
   (Z/X/C — Q/E stays altitude) · flares must LOOK like flares · **no shooting missiles
   backwards** · rear machine guns only on airframes that should have them.
8. **Accounts: authentication + database.** The front menu isn't enough — players need
   accounts that keep their record. (The science chooser question folded into this.)
9. **Weapon ownership UI** — pickups exist but with one slot there's no way to *see what you
   own/carry*; improve the inventory/loadout surface. Plus "the gun remembers" per-victim
   list (who this weapon killed) — verify what shipped, finish the rest.
10. **The fictional-world proof** — show the (fictional) president / government framing so
    nobody mistakes the countries for real-politics claims. Ties into MIS-1 and the press.
