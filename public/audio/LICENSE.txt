War World Sound Pack
====================
The .wav files shipped in this directory were generated with ElevenLabs'
text-to-sound-effects service (see tools/gen-sounds-ai.mjs and
tools/sound-specs.mjs for the prompts). They are used under the ElevenLabs
Terms of Service — https://elevenlabs.io/terms — per the generating account's
plan. They are NOT public-domain / CC0. Check your ElevenLabs plan's usage
rights before redistributing.

CC0 alternative
---------------
A fully procedural, CC0-licensed pack (no recordings, no third-party samples,
no external services) can be regenerated at any time with:

    npm run sounds        # tools/gen-sounds.mjs → overwrites this directory

That synthesized pack is dedicated to the public domain under CC0 1.0
(https://creativecommons.org/publicdomain/zero/1.0/) — see LICENSE-CC0.txt,
which applies to the synthesized output of `npm run sounds`, not to the
ElevenLabs files currently in this directory.
